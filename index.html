About PlusOne

PlusOne is a modern, fast-paced arithmetic game for iOS. Sharpen your mental math, test your memory, and compete globally—all in a sleek, immersive app experience.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I play? Memorize the starting number, then solve each operation in sequence. Each correct answer increases your score. Wrong answers subtract time!
  • How do I report a bug? Please email us at support@plusoneapp.com with details and screenshots if possible.
  • How do I reset my progress? Progress is stored locally. Delete and reinstall the app to reset, or contact us for help.
  • How do I access leaderboards? Sign in with Game Center from the main screen to view and compete on global leaderboards.
