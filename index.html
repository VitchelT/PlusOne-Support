About PlusOne

PlusOne is a modern, fast-paced arithmetic game for iOS. Sharpen your mental math, test your memory, and compete globally—all in a sleek, immersive app experience.

Getting Started

  • Download the app: Search “PlusOne” on the App Store.
  • Follow the walkthrough: Learn the rules of the game.
  • Start a game: Tap “Play” and begin solving problems. Speed, memory and accuracy matter!
  • (BONUS)Add a profile: Log in with Game Center to access leaderboards & future features.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I play? Memorize the starting number, then solve each operation in sequence. Each correct answer increases your score. Wrong answers subtract time!
  • How do I report a bug? Email plusoneappsupport@gmail.com with details and screenshots if possible.
  • Can I challenge friends? VS Mode is comming soon.
  • How do I access leaderboards? Sign in with Game Center to view global rankings.

Known Issues

  • Audio may stop after calls — we're working on a patch.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 7, 2025

Thank you for playing PlusOne. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our app. Please read this policy carefully. By using PlusOne, you agree to the terms described below.

1. Information We Collect

a. Personal Data

  • Game Center Information: If you sign in with Apple Game Center, we may access your Game Center ID, display name, and leaderboard scores. We do not access your email, contacts, or other personal identifiers.
  • In-App Purchases: Apple processes payment information. We do not receive or store your credit card or billing details.

b. Usage Data

  • Gameplay Statistics: We collect anonymized gameplay data (e.g., scores, session length, in-app purchase history) to improve the app and provide leaderboards.
  • Device Information: We may collect device type, OS version, and crash logs for debugging and analytics.

c. Local Data Storage

  • SwiftData: Your scores and progress are stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This data is not shared unless you opt into Game Center or backup your device.

d. Third-Party Advertising Partners

  • Ad Networks: PlusOne may display ads from third-party partners. These partners may collect device identifiers (such as IDFA), approximate location, and usage data to serve relevant ads. We do not share personally identifiable information with advertisers.
  • Data Shared with Ad Partners:
    • Device identifiers (IDFA, IP address)
    • App usage statistics
    • General location (city/region, not precise GPS)
  • Opt-Out: You can limit ad tracking in your device settings (Settings > Privacy > Advertising > Limit Ad Tracking).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve game features, leaderboards, and user experience
  • To process in-app purchases and restore purchases
  • To display relevant advertisements (if applicable)
  • To analyze app performance and fix bugs
  • To comply with legal obligations

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

  • Apple Services: Game Center and StoreKit data are processed by Apple in accordance with their privacy policies.
  • Third-Party Advertisers: We may share limited, non-personal data with ad partners for ad delivery and analytics.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights and users.

4. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. We recommend keeping your device updated and using strong passwords.

5. Your Rights and Choices

  • Access & Deletion: For Game Center or purchase data, please refer to Apple’s privacy tools.
  • Ad Preferences: You can adjust ad tracking preferences in your device settings.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted in the app and on our support page. Continued use of PlusOne after changes means you accept the new policy.
